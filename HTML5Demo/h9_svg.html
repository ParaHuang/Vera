for the path in svg, property d means draw

svg path: lines
command:
M: moveTo,start point, L: lineTo, H: horizontal lineTo, V: Vertical lineTo Z: cloth path

svg path: arc
command:
A rx ry xr laf sf x y
rx,ry:radius of x and y
xr: the rotate angle
laf:do you want to choose the longer arc, 1:yes , 0:no
sf:do you want to draw it clockwise, 1:yes , 0:no
x,y: x,y of the end point
